About the Artist

I have been involved in the art world from a young age. As I grew older, I began to specialize in skulls and skeletons, particularly using acrylic on canvas. My favorite pieces to make are bold, vibrant, pop-art-esque paintings on large or multiple canvases. Most of my art has been for myself, family members, or close friends, but I am always open to inquiries about custom commissions!
